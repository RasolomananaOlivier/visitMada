MAHAJANGA

Mahajanga, also known as Majunga, is nicknamed the city of flowers. The port and capital city of the northwest region of Madagascar, it offers a magnificent natural and cultural landscape. Situated at the mouth of the Betsiboka River, many people from all walks of life live together in peace and harmony, offering visitors an unforgettable stay. Many land and sea activities are possible. You won't be bored !

Best hotels

A mix of the charming, modern, and tried and true.

Antsanitia resort

Located in the northwest of the island, Antsanitia Resort Hotel Madagascar is nested in a tropical garden at the edge of the Indian Ocean. Certified Green Globe ® in 2013, the eco-resort lives in harmony with the fishing hamlets around it. Go back to the basics: the sun, the ocean, a long white sandy beach in Madagascar or a walk in the cool shade of the trees will revive your body and your soul.

Hotel Coco Lodge

Coco Lodge is a nice three-star hotel with pool, bar-lounge and restaurant, starting point for many excursions. Majunga, a cosmopolitan city, is a land that lives the culture of its population.

Lodge des Terres Blanches

Nestled in the heart of a private nature reserve, in an idyllic setting located 90km north of the city of Majunga, the boutique-hotel Lodge des Terres Blanches is located in a completely preserved environment where you access only by boat or private plane.

Karibu Lodge

In the residential district of La Corniche with a magnificent view of the sea, the Karibu Lodge has opened its doors since 03/2011. Built with a lot of taste with local materials: Mahajanga's size stone, granite of Tananarive...There is a lot of charm.

Can't-miss spots to dine, drink, and feast.

The restaurant Chez Madame Chabaud on the island of Katsepy is undoubtedly the address of the brand that has the most charm and exoticism. The restaurant has a park, and it also includes bungalows.

Best places

Places to see, ways to wander, and signature experiences.

National Park Ankarafantsika

Ankarafantsika includes a unique floristic training on mass sandy in which there are faunistic species at a very high endemic rate and not represented in other protected areas.

The Baobab

In the middle of the road throne a pluricentenary tree, a baobab of 800 years, a 14 m circumference, which makes the reputation of the city of Mahajanga, northwest of Madagascar.

Katsepy's lighthouse

At the mouth of the Canal of Mozambique and Betsiboka, you are nosing across the village of Katsepy and its lighthouse. Around the lighthouse you will surely fall on some curious lemurs, wild boars or even a crocodile farm.

Grand pavois

Grand Pavois is one of the best beaches in Madagascar. White sand, turquoise seas awaits you there. Several restaurants are around, you will not miss it.
